Rules and regulations-English

1. Scope of Application

  • 1.1 These rules apply to the entire Minecraft server, including all game modes and related services (e.g. Discord, events, tournaments).
  • 1.2 By joining the server, all players accept these rules as binding and agree to follow them.

2. In-Game Conduct

2.1 Any behavior that negatively impacts gameplay or other players is prohibited. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • 2.1.1 Disrupting teammates by blocking, trapping, or stealing shared items
  • 2.1.2 Unauthorized teaming or collusion in solo-based game modes
  • 2.1.3 Exploiting respawn situations (e.g. spawn camping)
  • 2.1.4 Intentionally delaying gameplay by hiding or going AFK
  • 2.1.5 Random or unjustified attacking in role-based modes
  • 2.1.6 Inappropriate or offensive builds and symbols

3. Modifications & Tools

3.1 The use of software or hardware that provides gameplay advantages is strictly forbidden, even if only passively active. Examples include:

  • 3.1.1 Combat mods like KillAura, Aimbot, AutoClicker
  • 3.1.2 Movement aids such as Fly, Speed, Glide, or Fast-Fall
  • 3.1.3 Informational mods like Damage Indicators, heart displays, player radar
  • 3.1.4 Automated inventory or armor management
  • 3.1.5 Manipulating game physics (e.g. custom knockback, NoSlow)
  • 3.1.6 Visual aids like X-Ray (including resource packs), transparent blocks, or visible nametags
  • 3.1.7 Clients that perform automatic actions (fighting, placing/breaking blocks, movement)
  • 3.1.8 Macros, multi-keybindings, or hardware tweaks to boost click rates

Use of VR, specialized hardware, or mods is at your own risk and must not offer any gameplay advantage.

4. Additional Provisions

  • 4.1 The Minecraft EULA is part of these server rules and must be followed.
  • 4.2 Using stolen, public, or compromised Minecraft accounts (e.g. alt accounts) is not allowed.
