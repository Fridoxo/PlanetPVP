Apply to join us

If you want to apply as a Moderator/Builder/Famous/Media, please create a ticket on our Discord server.

Creator Requirements — Famous High Tier
  • YouTube (Videos)
    2000 subscribers • 10,000 views/month • 1 video/month about PlanetPvP
  • YouTube (Shorts)
    10,000 subscribers • 3 shorts/month • 200,000 views/month
  • TikTok
    250,000 followers • 4 videos/month • 1,500,000 views/month
  • Twitch
    5,000 followers • at least 3 streams/month • 80 average viewers
Creator Requirements — Media Entry Tier
  • YouTube (Videos)
    500 subscribers • 3,000 views/month • 2 videos/month about PlanetPvP
  • YouTube (Shorts)
    2,500 subscribers • 5 shorts/month • 10,000 views/month
  • TikTok
    50,000 followers • 5 videos/month • 300,000 views/month
  • Twitch
    1,000 followers • 4 streams/month • 20 average viewers

Exceptions possible

JrModerator Application PlanetPVP

Please answer the following questions or provide the requested information:

  1. Why do you want to become a moderator at PlanetPVP?
  2. General information:
    • Your age
    • Languages you speak
    • Student / Trainee / University student / Employed?
    • Hobbies or interests outside of Minecraft (optional)
  3. How long have you been playing on our server?
    Type /playtime ingame to see your playtime.
  4. Do you already have experience with moderation in general and if so, what kind?
    All types of moderation, leadership, or organizational experience count, whether digital or real.
    e.g.: Discord/game server moderation (any game), forum moderation (e.g. Reddit), club leadership (e.g. chess, tech), class/school representative, youth group leader, event organization in a club (e.g. tournaments, festivals), team or shift leader, responsibility for IT systems or software projects at work...
  5. How much time per day can you usually spend on moderation?
    Excluding vacation, illness, etc.
  6. How would you react in a typical conflict situation between two players?
    Briefly describe how you would de-escalate and act according to the rules.
  7. Which rules are especially important to you and why?
  8. What sets you apart from other applicants?
    What are your special strengths or motivation?
  9. Are you currently active as a team member on other servers?
    If yes, where and in what role?

Builder Application PlanetPVP

Please answer the following questions or provide the requested information:

  1. Why do you want to join PlanetPVP as a builder?
  2. Since when have you been actively building in Minecraft?
    Singleplayer or multiplayer experience?
  3. Screenshots or videos of your previous projects
  4. General information (optional):
    • Your age

    • Hobbies or interests outside of Minecraft
